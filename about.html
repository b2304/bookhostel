About Us

Why created this website?


As long-term travellers ourselves, we are here to support all travel enthusiasts around the world in finding the most suitable hostel. Book My Hostel is started in 2020 because we saw a need for a better hostels guide website that really gives you all the information you need to find the absolute best hostels in the world.

Features of our website


This site offers comprehensive free listings for all hostels worldwide including:

  • Quick and easy searching
  • Direct contact information
  • Genuine unbiased reviews
  • Original hostel photographs
  • Insider knowledge & tips from other backpackers

Our system searches for the best prices and availability across all of the major booking systems. Save money, time and sanity.
